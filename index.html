Welcome to Vizag

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is the largest city in Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its beautiful beaches, serene landscape, and rich cultural heritage.

Nestled between the Eastern Ghats and the Bay of Bengal, Vizag offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and urban sophistication. From pristine beaches to ancient Buddhist sites, from lush green hills to a vibrant culinary scene - Vizag has something for everyone.

The city is also a major industrial hub and home to the Eastern Naval Command of the Indian Navy. But beyond its modern facade, Vizag retains an old-world charm that captivates visitors.

Must-Visit Places

Explore these breathtaking locations that make Vizag truly special

RK Beach

The most popular beach in Vizag, perfect for sunrise and sunset views with a lively beach road.

Kailasagiri

Hilltop park with panoramic city views, giant Shiva-Parvati statue, and lush gardens.

Borra Caves

Ancient limestone caves with stunning stalactite and stalagmite formations in Ananthagiri hills.

Expert Insights on Vizag

Discover why travel experts are captivated by the charm of Visakhapatnam.

Dr. Priya Sharma

"Vizag is a marvel of ecological harmony..."

Dr. Priya Sharma, a renowned environmental scientist, states, "Vizag is a marvel of ecological harmony, where urban development thoughtfully coexists with pristine natural landscapes. Its rich biodiversity, from the marine life along its coast to the verdant hills of the Eastern Ghats, makes it a unique case study for sustainable tourism. The city's commitment to preserving its natural beauty is truly commendable. It's a destination that perfectly balances urban sophistication with untouched natural splendor, offering visitors a holistic experience of beauty and sustainability."

Mr. Rajesh Kumar

"The culinary journey in Vizag is extraordinary..."

Mr. Rajesh Kumar, an acclaimed travel blogger and food critic, shares, "The culinary journey in Vizag is extraordinary, offering a delightful fusion of traditional Andhra flavors and fresh coastal seafood. From street food delicacies to fine dining experiences, every meal tells a story of the region's vibrant culture and abundant natural resources. It's a true paradise for gastronomes seeking authentic Indian tastes, truly a delight for the senses with its fresh catches and aromatic spices."

Ms. Anya Singh

"Vizag offers a unique blend of adventure..."

Ms. Anya Singh, an adventure tourism expert, remarks, "Vizag offers a unique blend of adventure and relaxation. Whether it's water sports at Rushikonda Beach, trekking in the scenic Araku Valley, or exploring the ancient Borra Caves, the city provides thrilling experiences for every adventurer. Its diverse landscape is an untapped potential for eco-tourism and outdoor activities, truly a hidden gem for those seeking thrill amidst nature's grandeur."
